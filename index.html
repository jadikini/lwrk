Welcome to tumu interiors

At TUMU INTERIORS,my goal is for you to wake up to your dream residential or commercial Interior Space. I am an Interior Designer and decorator who is able to take your wish and personality and transform it into a one-of-a-kind portrait of who you are, how you live, and what you love.

TUMU INTERIORS was created in 2015 from passion to see people enjoy the space they live and work in. Since then, my motive has been to reduce people's stress levels through enhancing the looks of people's homes and offices.

Creative designs are the result of meaningful dialogue and trust between the interior designer and client. INTERIOR AMENITIES


I believe that your outlook on life and peace can be improved,one room at a time.

Living room

This space was dressed up with inviting, homely sophisticated decor that is ready to impress. Because the space was a rental,it was important to the owner to have easy to clean,affordable and durable furnishing. The arrangement wisely utilised the space in the square room ,leaving room for smooth flow of movement,or even create a dance floor by removing the coffee table and arranging the furniture against the wall. Curtains

At this modern resort space, we created a soft, relaxing environment where all guests feel warm and invited. The colors of the window treatment were chosen to bring a relaxed and worry free mood to all guests. It brings out an aspect of purity and cleanliness which is very crucial in every resort or hotel.The result is a place of balance and beauty, in harmony with its surroundings. Do you love this look? Coffee shop

This lovely,small property has limited space,but that doesn't mean it cannot be fully functional and look good and spacious at the same time.I could see aesthetic potential in the blanc cement wall and floor room,immediately I set my eyes on it. This design was influenced by the commercial purpose of the space and the need for it to be an eye catching spot to attract customers. Wall paper on the accent wall and ceiling brought texture and space illusion in the tight space. Do you love this look? We can tailor a custom look just for you. Contact us today to find out how.
